# Offline Rain Sound

## The Problem
I needed a reliable way to play soothing white noise and rain sounds on my Facebook Portal Go. However, the Portal's browser is restrictive, and I couldn't rely on a stable internet connection in the nursery. 

I needed a solution that was:
1. **Offline-capable:** Once loaded, it stays loaded, even if the Wi-Fi drops.
2. **Simple:** Just play, pause, and switch between sounds. No bloat, no ads.

## Plot Twist
It's not just rain, in fact, there's only one rain sound in here. Sorry, project had pivot halfway through. Though the point was to have a "white noise" machine, which is also funny because there are no white noise in here either. 
* Rain

* Pink Noise
